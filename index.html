Infrastructure operations

Four nameservers are run under kria.tel:

  • a.ns.kria.tel at Lagrange Cloud Manchester
  • b.ns.kria.tel at Lagrange Cloud London
  • c.ns.kria.tel at SweHosting Stockholm
  • d.ns.kria.tel at Spaugur Data Reykjavík

Two autonomous systems are related to kria.tel:

  • ASN 51019 with PoPs in Reykjavík, London, Frankfurt, and Toronto
  • ASN 57851 with a PoP in Stockholm and globally anycasted by ASN 52025
